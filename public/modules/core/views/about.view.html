About Philos

As the name Philos (loving in greek) implies, sharing our passion stands at the heart of our mission. Whichever the way: conferences, workshops or consulting missions; we guide developers, technologists and agilists in their endeavors, we help them implement the best possible technologies and working methods for their task.

Technologies

At the center of Philos, lies our passion for technology. As tech aficionados, we love to explore the possibilities that technology give us.

Research & Development

We are constantly searching for new tools to experiment, new technologies to build with, in the hope of bringing those faster to the market and allow our partners to build better product and services.

Community

Philos helps a community of passionate developers and disrupters learn and share skills to bring the bleeding edge technologies and methods to the next level. Meeting up at conferences and events allows the community members to provide each other invaluable feedback on their respective work.
